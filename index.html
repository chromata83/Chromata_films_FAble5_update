— We're Kevin and Laura

This is not just a film. This is your legacy.

Partners in life for over 22 years, proud parents of two lovely boys ... and the co-founders of Chromata Films. What started as a shared passion became a full-service cinematography studio built on one obsession: making your wedding film feel like it belongs on the big screen.

Kevin brings a decade of VFX expertise and Vancouver Film School training behind the lens. Laura holds the vision together with a Master's in Finance and Communication ... managing every detail with elegance and warmth from first inquiry to final delivery.

We've spent nine years documenting love stories across France, Italy, Switzerland, and beyond. We take our craft seriously ... and we know how to have a good time doing it. We love to celebrate, and we genuinely love being part of your journey.

Our style lives at the intersection of cinematic storytelling, raw emotion, and that unmistakable French art de vivre ... bold, unique, and unashamedly extravagant when the moment calls for it. We don't follow trends. We set them.
